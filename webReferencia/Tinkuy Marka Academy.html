Hello! I'm Ines Kudo.

Nine years ago, I decided I would not settle for an education that prioritized conformity over independent thinking, excellence, and the joy of learning.

When my son entered the traditional system at just three years old, I watched the spark in his eyes begin to fade. He asked fewer questions. He did what he was told. And I found myself wondering: was this environment truly cultivating his full potential? Was it protecting his love of learning?

As a psychologist with over a decade of experience in international education policy, I thought I understood how learning worked. But it took my own child’s silence to open my eyes.

I didn’t want a system that trained him to comply. I wanted one that inspired excellence and protected his curiosity. One that built character as intentionally as it built intellect.

I was looking for a place where joy and rigor coexist. Where autonomy grows with accountability. Where young people pursue mastery with purpose.

That place existed, just not here.

So I brought it to Lima and joined the Acton Academy global network.

Learn More

I founded Tinkuy Marka Academy, and over the years it has grown into a close-knit community of families who share deep convictions about education and childhood.

Tinkuy is not a place where we simply send our children. It is a second home where we come together, hold one another to high standards, celebrate growth, and bring out the best in each other.

If you are intentionally raising your child to be kind, curious, and disciplined, to think deeply and love learning, we may be a good fit. I would be honored to meet you.

Enter your information in this form and I will personally send you our Info Kit, along with a link to schedule a visit.

While you’re here, I invite you to explore the principles that guide our work at Tinkuy.

Warmly,

Inés

Official Certification

 
 

Official National Certificates

All learners at Tinkuy Marka are officially registered in the Ministry of Education’s enrollment system (SIAGIE) through our partnership with Colegio PEAD Unidos.

PEAD can issue official certificates at any time upon a family’s request, for a small fee.

International Certification

Tinkuy also offers international certification options based on each family’s goals, including US transcripts through our IALDS accreditation (accepted by most colleges and universities in the US and worldwide), and K–12 high school certificates recognized internationally.

Some pathways also offer direct admission to selected colleges, universities, and institutes in Wisconsin, US.
